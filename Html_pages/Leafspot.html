Leaf Spot

Cause:

Leaf spot is caused by fungal or bacterial infections that affect the leaves of plants, leading to spots or lesions

Symptoms:

Dark, water-soaked spots appear on the leaves, often surrounded by yellow halos. These spots can cause premature leaf drop.

Prevention:

  • Water plants at the base to avoid wetting the leaves.
  • Ensure proper plant spacing to improve air circulation.
  • Apply appropriate fungicides or bactericides as needed.
