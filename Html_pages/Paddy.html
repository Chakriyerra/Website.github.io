Preventive steps for disease control in Paddy Plant:

1. Use Disease-Resistant Varieties:

  • Choose rice varieties that are resistant to common diseases like blast, sheath blight, and bacterial leaf blight. This can significantly reduce the risk of infection and enhance overall productivity.

    • 2. Seed Treatment:

  • Treat seeds with fungicides or biological agents like Trichoderma to prevent soil-borne diseases such as rice seedling rot
  • Soak seeds in a solution of water and fungicide for 24 hours before sowing to prevent early seedling diseases.

    • 3. Maintain Proper Spacing:

  • Ensure that paddy plants are spaced adequately to allow for good air circulation and reduce humidity around the plants. Crowded conditions promote fungal growth and disease transmission
  • Row spacing should be optimized to facilitate sunlight penetration and airflow
