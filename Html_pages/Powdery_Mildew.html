Powderydew

Cause:

This disease is caused by fungal infections that produce white, powdery spots on leaves, stems, and buds.

Symptoms:

The leaves, stems, and buds become covered in a white, powdery substance. These spots spread quickly, affecting the plant's growth

Prevention:

  • Ensure proper spacing between plants to improve airflow.
  • Remove affected parts immediately to avoid spreading the disease
  • Regularly apply fungicides to control further outbreaks
